Hello, I am Yuan-Fang Lin from Taipei, Taiwan!

I graduated from National Taiwan University and earned my Bachelor of Science degree in Electrical Engineering in January 2018. I am becoming a Master student majoring Computer Science in Columbia University in coming Fall. I am highly interested in and focus on the related fields of Machine Learning, especially on Deep Learning and its application on Computer Vision. You may take a look on my Resume tag for more experiences I had, or download it here.

A Programmer

Though majoring in Electrical Engineering, I joined the Speech Processing and Machine Learning Laboratory to start my journey in Machine Learning. Supervised by Prof. Hung-Yi Lee, I got myself familiar with Semantic Analysis, Speech Processing and so on. Furthermore, I believe in multi-discipline and am inspired by the applications of Machine Learning on Natural Language Processing. Therefore, I started my research on Computer Vision with Deep Learning in Prof. Yu-Chiang (Frank) Wang's Laboratory. I hope that, one day, I may contribute to the technology world.

A Photographer

Apart from my research and teaching experiences, I am a keen amateur photographer, to be specific, a Shutterbug. I enjoy capturing emotions around me with lens, no matter happiness, sorrowfulness, or even craziness. The smiling faces of viewers are my biggest rewards and motivation to do photography. I am often hired to provide high-quality pictures for events or competitions. I recognize those pictures from me as invaluable memories, and hope that one day I can capture all the wonderfulnesses worldwide in my viewpoint to share to the society.
