About Me.

Hello there ! I am Yuan-Fang Lin from Taipei, Taiwan. I major in Electrical Engineering in National Taiwan University. I will earn my Bachelor of Science degree in January, 2018. I am currently an undergraduate researcher devoting myself into Computer Science and teaching assistants of Algorithms and Machine Learning and Having it Deep and Structured. I am highly interested in and focus on the related fields of Machine Learning, especially on Deep Learning and its application, such as Computer Vision and Natural Language Processing.

Apart from my research and teaching experiences, I am a keen amateur photographer, to be specific, a Shutterbug. I enjoy capturing emotions around me with my lens, no matter happiness, sorrowfulness, or even craziness. The smiles on the faces of viewers are my biggest motivation to do photography. I participate lots of activities on or off campus and volunteer to provide high-quality pictures. I recognize those pictures from me as invaluable memories, and hope that one day I can capture all the wonderfulnesses worldwide in my viewpoint to share to the society.
